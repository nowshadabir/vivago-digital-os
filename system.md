Vivago Digital OS: Full Feature Blueprint
1. Project & Task Ecosystem
Kanban/Sprint Boards: Drag-and-drop task management with custom columns (e.g., Ideation, Design, Dev, QA, Deployment).

GitHub/GitLab Sync: Automatic task updates via webhooks (e.g., pushing code to main moves a task to "Live").

Time Tracking: A "Start Timer" button on tasks to calculate the actual labor cost per project.

The Vault: A secure, encrypted file manager for SRS docs, logo source files, and brand guidelines.

Milestone Roadmap: Visual progress tracking for long-term SaaS projects like LodgeOS.

2. Financial Intelligence (The Ledger)
Multi-Currency Invoicing: Generate professional invoices with automated tax/discount calculations.

Subscription Management: Automated recurring billing for your SaaS clients (monthly/yearly).

Expense & Burn Tracker: Log domain renewals, hosting fees, and salary payouts (e.g., for Rawnak Jahan).

Payment Gateway Bridge: Direct API integration with bKash, Nagad, and SSLCommerz for local clients.

Profitability Analytics: Real-time charts showing which projects are most profitable after expenses.

3. Client Relationship (CRM)
Client Profiles: Detailed records for clients (like Arif Sohel or Salahuddin Ahmed) with project history.

Magic Link Portals: Send a private link to clients where they can track progress without needing an account.

Credential Locker: Securely store SSH keys, DB logins, and WP-admin credentials using AES-256 encryption.

Lead Pipeline: A "Pre-Project" board to track potential deals from first contact to signed contract.

4. Team & Collaboration
Role-Based Access (RBAC): Custom permissions for your "brothers" like Saiful Imam Sani or other specialists.

Team Workload View: See who is overbooked and who is free at a glance.

Payroll & Bonus Bot: Automated generation of salary slips and performance-based bonus tracking.

5. Developer Utilities (The "Unique" Edge)
Vivago Snippet Library: A searchable repository for your team's reusable code blocks (PHP, Vue, CSS).

Uptime Sentinel: Integrated monitoring that pings your client sites (like ontomeel.com) and alerts you if they go down.

Command Palette (Cmd+K): A global search bar to jump to any invoice, project, or client in 2 seconds.

Agency Health Score: An AI-driven metric based on cash flow, project deadlines, and team efficiency.

6. Personal & Focus Features (DeepMe)
The Manuscript Layer: A distraction-free writing environment specifically for your book, সত্তা ও শূন্যতা.

Mental/Financial Goal Tracker: A private dashboard to track your personal recovery and growth milestones.

Mood Journaling: A quick-entry field to log daily thoughts mid-workday.

7. System & UI/UX

Mobile Web App (PWA): Fully responsive design so you can approve payments or check tasks from your phone.
Notification Engine: Slack or Email alerts for overdue invoices, completed tasks, or server downtime.
